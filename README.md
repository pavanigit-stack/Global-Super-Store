# Global-Super-Store
## Project Overview 
This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard built using the Global Superstore dataset (from kaggle) to analyse sales performance, profitability and regional trends. The dashboard helps stakeholders quickly identify key business insights such as top-performing regions, profitable product categories and sales trend over time.

The goal of this project is to practice data modelling, DAX calculations and data visualization to transform raw data into actionable insights.

## Key Performace Indicators (KPIs)

The dashboard focus on following primary keys:
1. **Total Sales**: Overall revenue generated from all the orders
2. **Total Profit**: Net Profit generated from sales transactions
3. **Total Orders**: Total number of orders placed by the customers
4. **Profit Margin**: Percentage of profit relative to total sales

## Features

- **Filter Panel**: Enables users to filter data by region, category, segment and order date
- **Sales Trend Analysis**: Visualizes sales performance over time to identify growth patterns and seasonal trends
- **Category and Sub-Category Analysis**: Displays sales and profit distribution across product categories and sub-categories
- **Regional Sales Analysis**: Compares sales and profit performance across different regions and countries
- **Customer Segment Analysis**: Breaks down sales contribution by customer segments such as Consumer, Corporate, and Home Office  
- **Profitability Insights**: Highlights high-performing and low-performing products based on profit

## Insights and conclusions

1. Sales performace varies significantly across regions, highlighting potential markets for growth.
2. Some product categories generate high revenue but lower profit margin.
3. Consumer and Corporate segment contribute to the majority of sales.
4. Certian sub-categories consistently outperform others in both sales and profitability.
5. Sales trennd reveak periods of high demand, which can help future business planning and inventory management.
